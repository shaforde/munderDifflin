About Us

We are a paper manufactering company who puts the customer first therefore putting out the BEST products! We understand that our customers love products that are uniques and made of the most durable materials possible so those are exactly the products we make. In 2018 our founder Paper Boy saw the need for an indutry leader that specializes in Origami paper. From that need came this powerhouse company reading to serve your every need. Our papers have been tested and inspected to ensure that no matter the project your paper will hold up. Our paper is also 100% recyclable to ensure that we are protecting our environment.
